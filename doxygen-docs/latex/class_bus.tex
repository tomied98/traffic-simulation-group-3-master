\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a bus in the simulation that follows a route with multiple stops. 

The {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_bus}{Bus}} class simulates a bus\textquotesingle{}s behavior, including its movement along a route with predefined stops. It extends the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_car}{Car}} class and supports the functionality to visit the next stop in its route. The bus moves at a defined speed and waits at each stop before moving to the next. The stops are cycled through in a loop, ensuring the bus continuously follows its route. The bus\textquotesingle{}s behavior also includes a waiting period at each stop before continuing.

The bus drives to the next stop, waits for a period (controlled by wait\+Time\+\_\+), and then updates the current stop index. The bus loops through the stops cyclically. \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<} The bus waits for 80 units of time after reaching a stop.
